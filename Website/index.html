About me

Hi! I'm Flavie!
I'm 22 years old and I'm in the last year of my master's degree in business school.
Currently living in Le Havre in an apartment, I can take care of your pets at my place or yours during your absence, come to visit them, come to walk them but also take care of their care if needed.
Having grown up with animals (cats, dogs, ferrets, fish, rabbits, guinea pigs, chickens, pigeons) I would be happy to take care of your loulou while waiting for your return.
See you soon I hope!
